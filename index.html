        Integrated Corrosion Computation - ICC

        About ICC

        Integrated Corrosion Computation (ICC) is a multidisciplinary corrosion research method that integrates a variety of simulation computing technologies, experimental data, and data science tools. Based on a multi-scale simulation method from micro-nano to meso-macro, it systematically studies the process from corrosion initiation to development, predicts the corrosion aging behavior of materials, and optimizes corrosion protection measures. This method not only helps to deeply understand the corrosion mechanism, but also accelerates the development of new materials and anti-corrosion technologies, and reduces the impact of corrosion on industry and society.
